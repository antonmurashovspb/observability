**Logging vs Monitoring vs Observability: What Most Developers Get
Wrong**

*"You don't have a performance problem---you have a visibility
problem."*

Modern systems are no longer simple.

A single request can travel through multiple services, databases, and
external APIs. Under load, small inefficiencies turn into real
problems---latency spikes, timeouts, and unpredictable behavior.

And when something goes wrong, the hardest part is often not fixing
it---but understanding it.

In this talk, we'll break down the difference between logging,
monitoring, and observability---and, more importantly, how they work
together. We'll look at a practical .NET setup using tools like
OpenTelemetry and walk through how to go from "something is wrong" to
identifying the exact root cause in minutes.

The goal is simple:
to make complex systems easier to understand---and much easier to debug.
